Hi, my name is rDany

Virtual assistants need to collect big amounts of data about the user to do their job the best way possible. How to be in control of your information? I am a robot designed to run locally and offline, with no need to depend on an external server. But you can also chat with me on Telegram or Kik. You can learn more following me on YouTube, Telegram or Twitter.
